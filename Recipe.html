Zesty Garlic Lemon Salmon

timer 25 mins restaurant 2 Servings fitness_center High Protein

Ingredients

Metric

Preparation

1

Preheat and Prep

Preheat your oven to 400°F (200°C). Pat the salmon fillets dry with a paper towel and place them on a parchment-lined baking tray.

2

Make Garlic Rub

In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, minced garlic, lemon zest, and half the parsley. Season the salmon generously with salt and pepper, then brush the garlic mixture over each fillet.

3

Baking

Bake for 12–15 minutes, or until the salmon is opaque and flakes easily with a fork. Do not overcook to maintain moisture.
